Dining

From the top floor to your room

Zeferan Restaurant

A magnificent venue with crystal amber-colored lightings and touches of Turkish-Islamic arts, Zeferan is situated on the top floor and offers panoramic views of the Old Town and the Sea of Marmara. The cuisine is predominantly Azerbaijani — a diverse range of rice dishes, vegetable and pickle roasts and an abundance of greens and grilled meat.
